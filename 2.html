Hypertext:

Hypertext is a text that contains links to other text.This word Hypertext was invented by Ted Nelson around 1965.Hypertext is text or text visible on a computer or any electronic device that has a hyperlink to another text,so that readers can access it immidiately. it is often clicked or a key is pressed to do this.
Today hypertext is used in every website. Each page has a link to other pages so that the user can easily reach the other page

Hypermedia

The word hypermedia is derived from Hypertext.Or is the ability to open new web pages by clicking on the link on the web browser. Hypermedia to create a non-network of information that is not based only one text,but may also include other media,such as images ,movies ,graphics and animation images,sound and video.
