MULTIMEDIA-APPLICATIONS

About-multimedia :

multimedia is a new concept emerged in the recent times.Now this technology is being widely used in web pages ,motion picture and intractive presentations ,animation etc.Multimedia has made a signficant impact in training/education,business presentations, public information access etc .This course intends to introduce and expose multimedia technology and various factors and features of authoring software.It will also help in making the internet application richer in content and presentations.

Introduction to multimedia:

As the name of multimedia ,multimedia is a combination of many medias.Multimedia includes many media such as text,audio,video,images,graphics animation etc. Multimedia is used to communicate information well in the computer.

Object of Multimedia:

The contents of multimedia can be expressed in many ways.Usually these contents are expressed by multimedia objects such as :- audio,video,text,animation,image and graphics.

  1. Text: Text is displayed in ASCII,UNICODE,HTML,Post script,PDF etc.this is the most common way of displaying information in multimedia
  2. Audio: Digital audio is an important content of multimedia .Audio in an application can be a simple sound ,music,speech,structure audio,etc.
  3. Video: An animation that includes audio is also called a video.
  4. Image: An image in multimedia can be just an image or it can be a sequence of framess
  5. Animation: An animation is a sequence of image frames that provide a continuum of illusion.
  6. Graphics: In computer science,graphics and images are considered the same,but graphics are a much larger subject in which drawing and manipulation are used.
