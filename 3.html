1.Cel Animation:

Cel animation is a type of traditional animation which is done by hand drawings.In this process many pictures are created which are very little different from each other but they are nature wise prograssive.Due to this prograssive nature, they are used to represent some action.A transparent sheet was used to trace these drawings.This transparent sheet is called cel and it is a kind of medium to draw the frames.To make the cel animations more atractive in current times, the drawings are accompanied by music,matching sound effect and correct timing ids used for each effect.
For example in a cartoon show, 10-12 frames are played with rapid succession per second to provid a movement to that cel animation.

2.Stop Animation:

Stop animation or Stop motion animation is a type of technique that allows objects to move on their own. In this some images are drawn in different positions and they are photographed separately.Pupperty is very prominent frame-to-frame animation type.Some Hollywood movies that have been animated via stop-motion effects are king kong , The Dinosaur ,and The Lost World.

3.Motion Graohics:

These motion graohics are pieces of digital footage or animation that create such an illusion in which it seems that they are in illusion motion and are also rotating .These are often combined with audio to give better shape and they are used in other multimedia projects.
These motion graphics are rarely used in animation like that frame-by-frame footage and animation you are using commonly. These motion graphics are not used in any story based like typical animation,but they are mostly used in animated abstract shapes and forms such as logos and logo elements.
