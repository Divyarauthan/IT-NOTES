Advantages of Animation:

Skill and ability Improvement:

Intractive animation makes it easy for children and adults to understand complex and diffcult things easily in a very short time.Along with this ,it also helps in increasing their skills and ability.

Intractivity:

Intractivity is a type of mutual action between the learner, the learner, the learning system,and the learning material.Learners can learn very quickly and in a fast way by watching these intractive videos,in which audio and video are used.

Engagement is high:

Intractive learning,which consists of live-action animation,simulation,video,audio,graphics,feedback,expert advice,and question and answers, creates interest in learners,due to which they engage more and watch things.

Flexibility and Safety:

Many things are quite dangerous such as real-life learning experiments that happen in physics and chemistry.Computer animation has been a good choice for all these:

  • Many experiments cannot be done in the workplace due to being more dangerous,but animated videos can be used to understand them.
  • Animation program can represent many such dangerous events without any hazards.
  • If a learner also makes a mistake or does not understand something,them he can repeat that experiment without getting injured.

motivation:

Since animation can be explained to people in a very inspring and interactive way,this is a better way to motivate people.

Eliminates Frustration:

Computer animation is so attractive that even difficult and complex things can be easily understood.This does not create any frustration in the mind of the learner.

Practically:

It is capable of presenting true-to-life situations that learners face every day.Adults are mostly practical learners-which means that they learn better by facing a real problem than them.
Computer animation also allows learners to learn by learn-by-viewing,learn-by-doing and learn-by-coaching.

Consistent:

All learners learn the same principle and skills.these help them to understand things in an organized way.

in this you can see such processes and things which are not possible to be shown practically. For example geological events such as eathqukes or erosion patterns.

Disadvantages of animation:

along with the advantages of all technology,there are also some disadvantages .So let's know about them further.

1. Some information is often lost in real-life learning animation program.The learner cannot practically learn things completely.

2.Computer animation programs may function properly from a technical point of view,but fitting them into the curriculum is not that easy.

3.To impliment computer animation properly,teachers must have some experience in programming technology.

4. It requires extensive memory and storage space to store these animation.

5.To play these animation properly,special equipment is required,which sometimes becomes more expensive.
